GAMEPLAY
As the General of the Unicorn Army you have 7 SPINS of the Battle Wheel.
The Heart Meter displays your odds of winning the battle and starts at 50%, evenly matched, though not for long!
If the wheel lands on a unicorn, you recieve 150 points and a kittehs becomes a unicorn, turning the battle in your favor!
If the wheel lands on a kitteh, you lose 150 points and a unicorn is turned into a kitteh clone, dwindling your forces.
If you land on the Golden Unicorn, you recieve 700 points and an instant win! The Laser Kitteh is vanquished!
LET THE BATTLE BEGIN!
